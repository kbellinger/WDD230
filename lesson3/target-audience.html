Target Audience

The target audience for this site is those within age range of 30-65 years old and have experience in business ownership. Website visitors would be local or new business owners wanting to contribute to the quality of life of the cumminuty members and to grow their business and connect with other business owners in the area.

Personas

Diana Henderson: Experienced Business Owner

Diana is 46 years old and the CEO of a retail store. She has a Masters in Business Admnistration and has been the CEO for the past 7 years. She is currently planning on building a store in the Skiatook area and wants to connect with other business owners in the area and understand the town's demographics better. She uses her MacBook when working and her iPhone 11. Because she is a busy woman she uses her phone frequently to visit sites while waiting in the school pickup line.

Samuel Thunderhawk

Samuel is a Skiatook local and has lived here his whole life. He is 53 years old and is the current owner of his family's boat rental and repair shop. He wants to continue growing his business and connections within the area. He is also of Native American and wishes to work with the community in perserving the history of Skiatook and the Osage. He doesn't so much business on his phone and prefers using a desktop or his Dell laptop.
