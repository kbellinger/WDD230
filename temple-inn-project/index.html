What we offer

Temple Shuttle

The temple shuttle makes routine stops to all four temples from Tuesday to Saturday. Find out specific times on the service page.>

Family History Center

We provide free access to a family history center that supports Family Search and printers for printing your own names for temple ordinance work.

Long-term Suites

Staying for an extended period of time? We provide long-term accommodations which include suites with a kitchenette and laundry card. This is a great option for full-time Temple Missionaries. Visit the service page or feel free to contact us for more information.

Changing facilities

Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who have traveled far and need to change to and from church clothes.

Warm Breakfast

Free, warm breakfast offered every morning.
