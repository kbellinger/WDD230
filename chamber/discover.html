Discover the Skiatook Lake area

Skiatook Lake

Skiatook Lake attracts thousands every spring and summer. It's a great spot for those that enjoy time out on the water fishing, sailing or swimming.

Downtown Skiatook

Visit and support local shops in historic downtown Skiatook

Central Park

Located at the Oasage Praries Trailhead, Skiatook Central Park has playground equipment, pavillions and beautiful walking paths.

Osage Casino and Hotel

Try your luck at the casino! Or get away at our hotel and enjoy a dip in the pool!

Native American History

Skiatook is known for being the place where the Osage Tribe and the Cherokee Tribe meet and has deep native american roots.

Skiatook Pioneer Day

Each year Skiatook celebrates with a festival including a parade, carnival rides, and vendors form all over.

Close to Downtown Tulsa

The excitement of Downtown Tulsa is an easy 25 minutes away.
